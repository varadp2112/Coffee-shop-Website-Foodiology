our menu popular menu

Latte

Latte is typically enjoyed as a morning or afternoon beverage, but it can be consumed at any time of the day. It's often chosen as a comforting and indulgent treat to savor during a break or social gathering.

Rs.399

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is best suited for individuals who enjoy a balanced combination of espresso, steamed milk, and foam, resulting in a creamy and indulgent coffee experience. It's particularly popular among those who appreciate the richness and depth of flavor that comes from the espresso, along with the creamy texture provided by the milk.

Rs.450

Americano

Americano is a simple beverage made by brewing coffee beans and water without adding any milk, cream, or sweeteners. It's enjoyed for its bold flavor and aroma, and when consumed in moderation.

Rs.399

Doppio

Doppio, which is simply a double shot of espresso, is best suited for individuals who prefer a strong and intense coffee flavor without the dilution of milk or other additives. It's ideal for those who enjoy the pure essence of coffee and desire a quick and potent caffeine boost.

Rs.469

Lungo

Lungo, a type of espresso made by using more water and allowing the shot to run longer, is suitable for individuals who prefer a milder coffee flavor with a smoother taste compared to traditional espresso. It's ideal for those who enjoy a longer coffee experience without the intensity of a regular espresso shot. Lungo is often favored by individuals who appreciate a more relaxed and nuanced coffee drinking experience.

Rs.499

Mocha

Mocha is suitable for individuals who enjoy the combination of coffee and chocolate flavors. It's a sweet and indulgent beverage that appeals to those with a sweet tooth or a craving for dessert-like coffee drinks. Mocha is often favored by people who enjoy the richness of chocolate combined with the boldness of coffee, making it a popular choice for a comforting treat.

Rs.599

Macchiatos

Macchiatos are popular among those who enjoy the bold flavor of espresso but also appreciate a touch of milk to mellow out the intensity. They're great for people who enjoy a balanced coffee experience, where the richness of the espresso is complemented by just a hint of creamy texture from the milk or foam. So, macchiatos are suitable for people who prefer a stronger coffee taste but still enjoy a touch of milkiness.

Rs.499

Flat Whites

Flat whites are often favored by coffee enthusiasts who appreciate a strong coffee flavor but also enjoy the smooth texture and velvety mouthfeel provided by the steamed milk. It's a great choice for those who prefer a more balanced coffee-to-milk ratio compared to a latte, as the flat white typically has a higher proportion of coffee to milk. People who enjoy the combination of bold espresso with the creaminess of steamed milk, without the excessive foam of a cappuccino, often appreciate flat whites.

Rs.499
